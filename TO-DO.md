## STUDENT [MAIN]
- Can Enroll
- Can View Grades
- Can Check Schedule 
- Can Check Announcements, Events and View Contacts
- Can Evaluate Professors

## FACULTY [MAIN]
- Can View and Encode Grades
- Can View past records of Workload
- Can Check assigned Schedule (Workload)
